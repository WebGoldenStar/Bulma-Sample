What is casual dating?

Not everyone is always looking for a steady relationship and great love. Sometimes you are freshly separated and do not want to bind yourself again, often you are just looking for some fun without obligations and not infrequently it does not work in the existing relationship or marriage and you want to spice up your love life a bit. If you find yourself here again, casual dating could be something for you. Casual dating is all about ... fun without obligation, friendship with certain advantages and discreet and anonymous adventures without consequences. Casual dating was developed for all women and men in search of a certain "kick" and is enjoying increasing popularity.
